Yoto Manager

Manage Elijah & Lev's Yoto cards and playlists in one place.

To connect, you need a Yoto Client ID — a short code that authorises this app to access your Yoto account. After entering it you'll be taken to Yoto's own login page where you sign in with your Yoto email and password (the same credentials you use in the Yoto app). Your token is stored locally in this browser and never leaves your computer.
